A little bit about me...

Hello! My name is Christopher Noah George Cruz (but most people just call me Chris, for short).

I am an up-and-coming programmer currently studying for my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at University of California, Santa Cruz. My journey as a computer programmer didn't start until recently. My original love was for music and upon further discussion with myself, I decided to try and commit to my dream (as should everyone). After multiple years of trying to make it work, going from place to place, show to show, nothing really panned out the way that I dreamed it would. Most people would see this as a waste of time and energy, but I didn't. If anything, I believe that this avenue that I decided to pursue prior to my journey into computer science has helped guide me and set the foundations for what I really needed to progress further. I transferred that same determination into my education and work ethic and ultimately, it propelled me to go even further into this field. As a kid, I always had a love for technology, software, video games, and the internet and that is what's keeping me going throughout this roller-coaster of a journey. Often times, with programming, one will hit a wall, and it can be difficult to keep your mental perseverance fortified, but what I had learned from previous experience in music, is that even when your situation is not ideal or where you pictured it would be, do not let that deter you from achieving your dreams. Find your passion, your dream, and just go for it, no matter the costs. That passion will ultimately get you through whatever comes your way as it has for me. Join me in my journey into computer science and programming and hopefully you can see the potential of my passion that I have for this extraordinary field!
